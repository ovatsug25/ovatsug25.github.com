Marketing is really very simple.

  1. Do something that is really awesome, that you like and are proud of.
  2. Tell other people why they want to hear/buy/talk about it.

Most people think that there is a trick to getting customers, donors, fans, etc.

There really is no trick other than to do something really awesome and tell people about it like you would to your best friend.

Back in the day, marketing used to mean soemthing a little different. Before the internet, getting your product into a market was actually pretty tough and costly. You couldn’t just find a phone number and contact a store in Baton Rouge if you were in New Orleans. It would take much more work than just a Google search. In this day and age, most communication and distribution channels are completley free and even more effective than they were previously. The blog that you should be keeping here should do.

In terms of the internet all you really have to do is make something cool and make a YouTube video about it which you will then post on every social netowrking website from LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube, Reddit, Your Blog, Hacker News and so forth. If you’re good, millions and millions of people will hear about it.

That doesn’t mean we are done with the old world style of cold-calling and getting peole to like you just yet. You will still have to do that to get inside large corporations and such. Even if you have a really famous YouTube video. This is the focus of other question however.

Good marketers focus on making their robot good.
