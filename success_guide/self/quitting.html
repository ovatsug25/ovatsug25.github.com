Quitting can be a good or a bad decision.

It will be important to learn how to distinguish between a good and a bad quit.

Oftentimes, you feel like you are stuck. Like you can’t do anything else. A lot of those times, you will be close to a very to a big breakthrough.

You just have to continue to push through it. And if you are courageous and persistent enough you will.

Sometimes you will quit. And you shouldn’t have. Those are your muscles building up tolerance to difficulty.

Sometimes though, you will quit for the right reasons. Many curious people have a long running list of things they want to be able to do and master. Oftentimes, a long list will lead to mediocrity.

Quitting on all but one of those items is probably a good thing. People only care about really excellent work, so it will often be more valuable to focus on that one thing.

If you have already found your thing, don’t quit because it is hard though. Embrace the climb and realize that when the climb is difficult, the view from the peak is often much more beautiful.
